The Lakdiwala Legacy

Founded in 2015 by visionary entrepreneurs Akram Hussain and Sunil Kumar in the heart of Hyderabad, Telangana, Lakdiwala has emerged as a beacon of excellence in the timber industry. What began as a modest venture has transformed into a prominent Indian manufacturer and supplier of premium quality Teak Wood Products, with operations extending to the UAE as Best Noble Timber Trading.

With unwavering dedication and years of market expertise, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the timber industry, serving clients across the globe with the finest quality products. We are proud suppliers of premium teak wood from three distinguished sources: Burma Teak, African Teak, and Indian Teak, each offering unique characteristics and exceptional quality for diverse applications.

Our Products & Services

Premium Teak Logs

We specialize in sourcing and supplying high-quality teak logs from trusted suppliers across India. Our logs are carefully selected for their straight grain, natural oil content, and durability. Perfect for furniture making and construction projects.

  • Quality graded
  • Natural durability
  • Ready for processing

Teak Planks & Slabs

Our teak planks and slabs are processed using traditional woodworking techniques combined with modern machinery. Available in various thicknesses and finishes for different applications including furniture, flooring, and construction.

  • Custom sizes
  • Multiple grades
  • Ready for use

Custom Timber Solutions

We provide customized timber solutions for furniture manufacturers, interior designers, and construction companies. From raw logs to finished products, we handle every step of the process with quality assurance.

  • Custom cutting
  • Quality guarantee
  • Timely delivery

Premium Indian Teak Wood

We also sell premium Indian teakwood, particularly from the Central Provinces (CP) and Balarsha regions. Our Indian teak can be used for doors, frames, furniture, and architectural joinery. Sourced directly from Indian forests with natural durability and beautiful grain patterns.

  • CP & Balarsha origin
  • Architectural grade
  • Natural grain patterns
